UCD Rowing club
Founded in 1917 ucd rowing club is open to all students and public by invitation. The largest boat house on the river liffy , ucd boat house has one of the largest and most diverse boat type. Ucd boat house has several main dvisions , Mens and womens being the largest of the club subdivision The main focus is however on racing and intermediate rowing.Be prepared for long hours of intensive sessions

Neptune Rowing club
Condition:18+ only Over the years, the Club has produced world champions, many national champions and have often been the backbone of the Irish International Rowing Squad.It provides a modern, spacious training facility and a friendly, inclusive and supportive environment for athlets of all skill levels. Neptune Rowing Club is one of the foremost rowing clubs in Ireland. The Club was founded in 1908, so well into its second century so I take it that they have some experience. The Club is dedicated to promoting the sport of rowing and achieving excellence in rowing competition.
